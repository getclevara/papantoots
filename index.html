Who We Are

Family Kitchen,
Community Table

Papa N Toots started the way the best things do around here — cooking for family, then for friends, then for the whole block. Now we cater events all across Hawaiʻi Island, and every plate still gets the same care as if we were feeding our own.

We don't do shortcuts. Korean Chicken gets that glaze right. Pastelle Sausage gets smoked slow. Pickled goods get jarred by hand. If it's got our name on it, it's done the way it's supposed to be done.

Rooted on the Big Island

Based in Keaʻau, cooking out of a licensed commercial kitchen. We serve the whole island — Hilo side, Kona side, and everything in between.

Everything from Scratch

Seasonings blended by hand. Sausage slow-smoked the traditional way. Every plate built with the same care whether it's ten people or two hundred.

For Every Gathering

Grad parties, baby lūʻau, work functions, celebrations of life. When your people are coming together, we make sure the food is right.
